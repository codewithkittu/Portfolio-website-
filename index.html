Smart Agriculture Using IOT

College Last Year Project

In our final year project, we developed a smart agriculture system using Python and IoT technology, incorporating four sensors and a buzzer. The system featured smoke and fire sensors for detecting potential hazards, soil moisture sensors to monitor and manage irrigation needs, temperature sensors to track environmental conditions, and water sensors to ensure optimal water levels. By integrating these sensors, our system provided real-time data and automated alerts, facilitating efficient farm management. This innovative approach aimed to enhance crop productivity, reduce resource wastage, and ensure a safer, more sustainable agricultural environment.
